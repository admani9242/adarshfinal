SoundScaped is a website that features a collection of interesting and unique sound textures and ambiences recorded around Toronto. The curator describes themselves as a sound detective who captures hidden sounds in daily life. The collection includes sounds like streetcars and leaves rustling in the wind, and is suitable for sound designers or anyone interested in audio. The website invites visitors to explore the collection and reminds them to use headphones to avoid disturbing the neighbors.

For the best experience please connect to an external audio device (Earphones, Headphones, Speakers) before proceeding.
